Frequently Asked Questions

To apply for housing with Properties 4 Creations, simply visit our Apply Now page and fill out our secure online application form. We'll ask for basic information about your housing needs, household size, and voucher type. Our team reviews applications within 24-48 hours and contacts you to discuss available properties that match your requirements.

We accept both Section 8 and HUD-VASH vouchers, and our veteran-owned company specializes in working with military families and veterans.

Yes, we proudly accept Section 8 vouchers at all our properties. As a veteran-owned housing provider, we understand the importance of making quality housing accessible to families who need assistance.

All our properties are Section 8 approved, and we work directly with local housing authorities to ensure a smooth voucher processing experience. We never charge additional fees beyond standard security deposits and rent amounts.

We are certified HUD-VASH partners and have dedicated properties specifically designated for veterans with HUD-VASH vouchers. Our veteran-owned company was founded by those who understand the unique housing challenges faced by military families.

HUD-VASH (Housing and Urban Development - Veterans Affairs Supportive Housing) is a program that combines rental assistance with case management services. We work closely with VA case managers to ensure our veterans receive the comprehensive support they need.

Yes, we welcome pets at most of our properties! Our pet policy includes:

  • Pet Deposit: $300 (refundable with proper care of the property)
  • Monthly Pet Rent: $25 per pet
  • Size Limit: No weight restrictions for service animals
  • Breed Restrictions: None for service animals; case-by-case for others

Service animals and emotional support animals are always welcome with proper documentation. We understand pets are family members and work to accommodate responsible pet owners.

Income requirements vary based on your voucher program and household size:

  • Section 8: Must meet program income limits (typically 30-50% of area median income)
  • HUD-VASH: Determined by VA case manager and program guidelines
  • Market Rate: 2.5x monthly rent minimum (may be flexible for qualified applicants)

We work with various income sources including employment, disability benefits, Social Security, and veterans benefits. Our team can help you understand eligibility and explore assistance programs that may help bridge any gaps.
