Our Compliance Commitment

At Properties 4 Creations, we are committed to the highest standards of transparency, compliance, and ethical practices. As a veteran-owned business serving vulnerable populations, we understand the importance of maintaining trust through open communication and strict adherence to all applicable laws and regulations.

Our Core Principles:

  • Transparency: Clear communication about policies, procedures, and expectations
  • Compliance: Strict adherence to federal, state, and local housing regulations
  • Accountability: Taking responsibility for our actions and decisions
  • Resident Rights: Protecting and respecting the rights of all residents
  • Quality Standards: Maintaining high standards for property conditions and management

Resident Rights

We are committed to protecting and respecting the rights of all our residents. Here are the key rights you have as a resident:

Your Rights Include:

  • Safe Housing: The right to live in a safe, habitable home that meets health and safety standards
  • Privacy: The right to privacy in your home, with proper notice for any entry
  • Non-Discrimination: The right to be treated fairly without discrimination
  • Due Process: The right to proper notice and opportunity to respond to any lease violations
  • Security Deposit: The right to proper handling and return of your security deposit
  • Repair Requests: The right to have repair requests addressed in a timely manner
  • Reasonable Accommodations: The right to request reasonable accommodations for disabilities
  • Quiet Enjoyment: The right to enjoy your home without unreasonable interference

Quality Standards

Property Inspections

Regular inspections to ensure properties meet HUD Housing Quality Standards (HQS) and local code requirements.

  • Initial move-in inspections
  • Annual recertification inspections
  • Repair follow-up inspections
  • Emergency repair assessments

Maintenance Standards

Commitment to maintaining properties in excellent condition with responsive repair services.

  • 24-hour emergency repair response
  • 48-hour standard repair response
  • Regular preventative maintenance
  • Seasonal property checks

Rent Reasonableness

All rents are determined through market analysis to ensure they are reasonable and competitive.

  • Comparative market analysis
  • Annual rent reviews
  • HUD approval for voucher programs
  • Transparent rent calculations

Reporting & Accountability

We maintain transparency through regular reporting and open communication channels. Our accountability measures include:

Accountability Measures:

  • Regular Audits: Internal and external audits of compliance practices
  • Resident Surveys: Regular feedback collection from residents
  • Performance Metrics: Tracking of key performance indicators
  • Compliance Training: Ongoing training for all staff members
  • Third-Party Reviews: Independent reviews of our practices

How to Report Concerns:

If you have any concerns about our practices or need to report an issue:

  • Contact our compliance officer at compliance@properties4creations.com
  • Call our main office and ask to speak with management
  • Submit a written complaint to our office address
  • Report violations to appropriate government agencies
