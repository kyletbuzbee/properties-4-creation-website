Transparency

Learn about our renovation standards, cost transparency, and warranties at Properties 4 Creations.

Renovation Standards

Every property undergoes extensive renovations to ensure quality and comfort. Our renovations include:

  • HVAC systems
  • Roofing
  • Plumbing
  • Electrical
  • Cosmetic updates

Cost Transparency

We invest over $30,000 in each property to provide a safe and comfortable living environment. This includes:

  • HVAC systems: $5,000 - $7,000
  • Roofing: $5,000 - $10,000
  • Plumbing: $2,000 - $5,000
  • Electrical: $2,000 - $5,000
  • Cosmetic updates: $5,000 - $10,000

Warranty Information

Our properties come with comprehensive warranties for:

  • HVAC systems: 1 year
  • Roofing: 5 years
  • Plumbing: 1 year
  • Electrical: 1 year

Inspection Reports

All properties undergo thorough inspections to ensure they meet our high standards. Inspection reports are available upon request.

Fair Housing Compliance

We are committed to fair housing practices and comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
