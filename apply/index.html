Common Application Questions

How long does the application process take?

We review applications within 24-48 hours and can typically match you with a property within 7-14 days.

What documents do I need?

Voucher approval letter, ID, proof of income, and rental history. We'll guide you through the specific requirements.

Are there any fees?

No application fees. We only charge standard deposits and rent as required by your voucher program.
